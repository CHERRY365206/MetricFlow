Welcome to MetricFlow

MetricFlow is an enterprise goal-setting and tracking portal designed for seamless performance management.

👤 For Employees

  • Define goals aligned with 4 thrust areas (Financial, Customer, Process, Learning).
  • Ensure total weightage equals exactly 100% before submission.
  • Update your actual progress during active check-in cycles (Q1, Q2, etc.).

👥 For Managers

  • Review, approve, or return employee goals for rework.
  • Conduct quarterly check-ins and provide feedback on approved goals.
  • Export your team's performance data to CSV.

⚙️ For HR / Admins

  • Control the active system cycle (locking/unlocking features).
  • Monitor real-time analytics and goal distribution.
  • Run the Escalation Engine to automatically nudge managers and employees via Teams/Email.

Select a role from the dropdown on the login screen to explore!
